Vegetables

Venues where vegetables are provided for our supermarket

Nuwaraeliya farm

Nuwara Eliya; is well-known for it's climate and fertile soil, which is suitable for cultivating a variety of vegetables. The Nuwara Eliya Market, which is open every day, is a major provider of fresh, high-quality vegetables to supermarkets around the country.

Dambulla market

Dambulla market is one of Sri Lanka's main wholesale vegetable markets. It serves as an important distribution point for veggies to supermarkets and other retail outlets throughout the country.

Pettah market

Pettah market located in Colombo; is one of Sri Lanka's oldest and biggest wholesale marketplaces, acting as a major distribution hub for vegetables from all over the nation.
